Room Styling

Found For You offers two styling options to help you decorate your space:

Standard Room Plan (€5): You already have a Pinterest board with inspiration. Based on your answers in the form, I’ll send back direct shopping links to matching products and decor.

Premium Room Plan (€10): You don’t have a Pinterest board? No problem! I’ll create a moodboard based on your form answers. After you approve the moodboard, I’ll send you direct shopping links and additional inspiration images.

After submitting the form, you'll receive a payment request via email or Instagram within 24 hours. Once payment is confirmed:

  • Standard Plan: You'll receive your results within 2–5 days.
  • Premium Plan: You'll receive everything within 2–10 days (depending on moodboard approval).
